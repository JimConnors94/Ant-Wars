Worker ants excel at gathering food, but add little to the colonies defensive or offensive capabilities.

Warrior and guard ants eat far more food than they gather, but are needed to increase your stats.

Warriors specialize in attack, while Guards specialize in defence.

Hatch the right balance of ants to succeed.

If your run out of food, your ants will starve.

Every 30 seconds your enemy will attempt to raid you and kill your ants.

After an enemy attempts a raid, you'll be able to strike back.
